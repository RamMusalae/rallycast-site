RallyCast

Weather Intelligence for Court Sports

Know before you play.

RallyCast analyzes wind, rain chance, temperature, and humidity to produce a simple playability decision for tennis or pickleball.

  • Single weather score from 0-100
  • Fast result for your selected location, date, and time
  • Forecast support up to 10 days ahead
  • Readable summary with key conditions in one line
  • Excellent (85-100)
  • Good (65-84)
  • Maybe (45-64)
  • No (0-44)

Core weather factors: wind speed, rain probability, heat/cold, humidity.
